30 Days of Sentence Writing - Day 29

Posted on 2021-07-07 Edited on 2021-07-19 In English Views:

As rich countries loosen lockdowns, an economic concern is emerging.

However, pundits do not agree on whether stimulus handouts really lead people to shirk.

[分数] [4/5]

S01

分析

As rich countries loosen lockdowns, an economic concern is emerging.

随着发达国家放松封锁，一个经济谜团正在浮现。

【结构】as …, a concern is emerging

【解析】

[主干]

an economic puzzle is emerging.

[修饰主干的部分]

时间状语从句：As rich countries loosen lockdowns 是由 as 引导的时间状语从句。这整个句子虽然简单，但 as …, a concern is emerging 这个句型在各类英文话题写作中却非常实用，值得积累。

仿写

科技话题

  • 中文：

    随着现代人越来越依赖高科技产品，一个社会性的担忧正在浮现。

  • 参考译文：

    As modern people become increasingly dependent on high-tech products, a societal concern is emergint.

  • 我的回答：

    As high-tech products are become reliable for modern people, a socialble concern is emerging.

  • 评价：

    are becoming

环境话题

  • 中文：

    随着工业化的不断推进，一个关于环境保护的担忧正在浮现。

  • 参考译文：

    As industrialization continues to advance, a concern about environmental protection is emerging.

  • 我的回答：

    As industrialization proceeding, a environmental concern is emerging.

  • 评价：

    as 引导时间状语从句 As industrialization continues to advance/proceed

文化传统话题

  • 中文：

    随着年轻人越来越崇尚外来文化，一个关于保护传统文化传承的担忧正在浮现。

  • 参考译文：

    As young people increasingly revere foreign cultures, a concern about preserving our traditional culture is emerging.

  • 我的回答：

    As young people more interesting in foreign cultures, a concern about protecting traditional culture is emerging.

  • 评价：

    As young people become more interested in foreign cultures.

个人品质话题

  • 中文：

    随着家长和老师把主要精力放在提高学生考试分数上，一个关于学生能否适应真实世界的担忧正在浮现。

  • 参考译文：

    As parents and teachers focus primarily on improving students’ test scores, a concern about students’ ability to adapt to the real world is emerging.

  • 我的回答：

    As parents and teachers focused on bringing students’ exam scores up, a concern about whether they can get used to reality is emerging.

  • 评价：

    一般现在时即可 - focus

S02

分析

However, pundits do not agree on whether stimulus handouts really lead people to shirk.

然而，对于刺激计划是否真的会使人懒惰，专家们意见不一。

【结构】A do not agree on whether …

【解析】

pundits do not agree on …, 对于 … 专家们意见不一

宾语从句 whether stimulus handouts really lead people to shirk，作 agree on 的宾语，lead people to do sth. 令人做某事

stimulus handouts to shirk 作宾语 people 的宾语补足语

仿写

科技话题

  • 中文：

    对于智能手机是否真的会伤害孩子的社交能力，家长们意见不一。

  • 参考译文：

    Parents do not agree on whether smartphones can really harm their children’s social skills.

  • 我的回答：

    Parents do not agree on whether smartphones really undermine children’s social ability.

  • 评价：

    nice

环境话题

  • 中文：

    对于限制汽车通行是否真的能够提升空气质量，市民们意见不一。

  • 参考译文：

    Citizens do not agree on whether reducing car use can really improve air quality.

  • 我的回答：

    Citizens do not agree on whether restriction on movements will improve air quality.

  • 评价：

    agree on whether restriction on car usi

文化传统话题

  • 中文：

    对于加强在媒体平台上宣传传统能否吸引年轻人喜欢上传统文化，网民们意见不一。

  • 参考译文：

    Netizens do not agree on whether promoting traditional culture on media platforms can make young people become interested in it.

  • 我的回答：

    Netizens do not agree on whether more publicity about traditional culture will make young people interested in it.

  • 评价：

    nice

个人品质话题

  • 中文：

    对于赚到很多钱能否让人长时间地感到幸福，心理学家们意见不一。

  • 参考译文：

    Psychologists do not agree on whether earning a great deal of money can increase people’s happiness for long periods of time.

  • 我的回答：

    Psychologists do not agree on whether making more money help people get a long-term happiness.

  • 评价：

    注意主谓一致 making more money helps
